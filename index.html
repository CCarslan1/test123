About Us

The British Alevi Federation is an umbrella organization for approximately 300,000 Alevis living in the United Kingdom. We support 17 Alevi Cultural Centers across the UK, including locations such as Wood Green (London), Glasgow, Leicester, and Manchester.

What is Alevism?

Alevism centers on the belief that every human being is a carrier of the essence of God. Alevis consider everything as sacred and as carriers of an essence from God. Core principles include equality, respect, and community.

  • Respectful behavior is expected at all times.
  • Alevis consider the cosmos and humanity in a state of total unity.
  • Women and men are equal within Alevi practices.
